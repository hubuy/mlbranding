01 / BRAND STRATEGY
Positioning

The art of the soft game

The Metropolitan League is New York's premier pickleball league — all five boroughs under one banner. The Dink identity is built on the sport's signature skill: the soft, surgical shot at the kitchen line. It's a brand for players who know that touch beats power — confident, precise, and a little bit cheeky, with the polish of a top-flight league.

Construction & Clear Space

Clear space & minimum size

The emblem is built on a circular grid; the arc springs from the lower-left contact point to a ball one ball-diameter inside the keyline. Keep clear space equal to the ball's diameter on all sides, and never redraw it freehand — always place the master art.
